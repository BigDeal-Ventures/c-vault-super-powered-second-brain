# C-Vault Examples

Use these examples as starting points. Direct slash commands are most deterministic. Natural asks route through `using-c-vault` when available.

## Core Examples

- `/daily-review` - Review today, identify open loops, and choose next actions.
- `/weekly-synthesis` - Synthesize the week and prepare next week.
- `/inbox-processor` - Process an inbox note and decide where it belongs.
- `/thinking-partner` - Think through a messy idea, decision, or tradeoff.
- `/de-ai-ify` - Make writing sound more grounded and human.

## Core Natural Asks

- Synthesize this week's notes.
- Help me think through this product idea.
- Clean this inbox note and suggest where it belongs.
- Make this writing sound less AI.

## CMO Skills Examples

- `/cm-research` - Research ICPs, competitors, and market context.
- `/cm-position` - Run a full positioning workshop.
- `content-strategy` - Build a content plan or editorial calendar.
- `copywriting` - Draft landing page, email, or ad copy.
- Plan content for this product.
- Research competitors and ICP for this category.
- Improve this homepage copy for conversion.
