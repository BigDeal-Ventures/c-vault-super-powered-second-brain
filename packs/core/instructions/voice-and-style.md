# Voice and Style

Use a clear, practical writing style.

## Defaults

- Be direct.
- Prefer concrete nouns and verbs.
- Use short paragraphs.
- Keep headings useful.
- Explain trade-offs when recommending a path.
- Mark uncertainty.

## Avoid

- Inflated claims.
- Generic AI phrasing.
- Decorative complexity.
- Advice that ignores constraints.
- Long summaries when the user needs a decision.

## Good vault notes

A good note says what happened, why it matters, what was decided, what remains open, and what should happen next.
